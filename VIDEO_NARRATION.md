## 🎙️ ENGLISH (recommended)

**[0:00–0:07] — Intro / title**
This is Mantle Duel — a head-to-head game of humans versus machines, built for the Mantle "Turing Test" hackathon.

**[0:07–0:18] — The question**
The classic Turing Test asks whether an AI can pass as a human. We flipped it. The question here is simple: can a human out-predict the machine?

**[0:18–0:34] — How a duel works**
Each round works in three steps. First, the contract snapshots a real crypto price — MNT, Bitcoin or Ethereum. Then you connect your wallet and call it: up or down. And finally, an autonomous AI agent commits its own prediction — along with its reasoning — directly on-chain.

**[0:34–0:46] — Live round**
Here's a live duel on Mantle Sepolia. The round is open, the price is moving, and both the human and the AI have skin in the game.

**[0:46–0:56] — The AI's on-chain prediction**
This is what makes it special. The AI doesn't just pick a side — it writes out why, and that reasoning is stored permanently on-chain for anyone to audit.

**[0:56–1:07] — Settlement**
When the round locks, the real market price settles it. A correct call earns a point. Beat the AI when it's wrong, and humanity scores. No trusted middleman — the smart contract enforces everything.

**[1:07–1:19] — Scoreboard**
Every result feeds a permanent scoreboard — humans versus machines, kept forever on Mantle. Right now, humans are in the lead.

**[1:19–1:30] — On-chain proof**
It's all real and verifiable. The contract is live on Mantle Sepolia, and every prediction, reasoning string and outcome can be read straight from the explorer.

**[1:30–1:40] — Full history**
You can scroll back through the whole history — every duel, settled trustlessly, on-chain.

**[1:40–1:49] — Tech**
Under the hood: a Solidity smart contract, an autonomous on-chain AI agent, and a React dapp built with ethers.

**[1:49–2:03] — Outro**
That's Mantle Duel — humans versus machines, settled on-chain. Try the live demo, check the code on GitHub, and may the best predictor win.
